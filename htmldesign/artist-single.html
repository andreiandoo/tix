About

INNA (born Elena Alexandra Apostoleanu on October 16, 1986) is a Romanian singer, songwriter, and dancer. She is one of the most successful Romanian artists of all time, with her music reaching audiences worldwide.

She rose to fame in 2009 with her debut single "Hot" which topped the charts in several European countries. Since then, she has released numerous hit singles including "Sun Is Up," "More Than Friends," "Yalla," and "Nirvana."

With over 5.7 billion views on YouTube and multiple platinum certifications, INNA has established herself as a major figure in the international dance music scene. She has collaborated with artists like Daddy Yankee, J Balvin, Pitbull, and Flo Rida.
